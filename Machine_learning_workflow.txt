In the realm of machine learning, a well-structured workflow is paramount to achieving desirable outcomes.
A crucial element of this workflow is the order in which the steps are executed. By rearranging the order 
of the following steps, you will gain a deeper understanding of their significance and their role in a
typical machine learning project.

The workflow is

1. Requirements Gathering
2. Data Preparation
3. Model Development
4. Model Evaluation and Tuning
5. Model Testing
6. Model Deployment
